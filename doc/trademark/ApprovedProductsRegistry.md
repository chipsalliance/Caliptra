# Approved Caliptra Trademark Targets of Evaluation (TOEs)

This document maintains the official registry of Targets of Evaluation (TOEs) that have been
successfully audited and approved for Caliptra trademark compliance. Each listed product has
undergone evaluation by an accredited third-party laboratory following the
[Caliptra Checklist and Evaluation Methodology](CaliptraChecklistAndEvaluationMethodology.md)
to ensure secure silicon root of trust integration. The evaluation results are reviewed and
granted by the Caliptra Trademark Owner (TAC) in accordance with the
[Caliptra Trademark Policy](CaliptraTrademarkPolicy.md) and the established
[Caliptra Trademark Audit Process](CaliptraTrademarkAuditProcess.md).
